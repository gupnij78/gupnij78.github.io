Clubs & Teams

Enver Creek Secondary Has a Variety of Teams. Students are encouraged to take part in extra-cirricular activities, as they will help students stay on track. Most importantly, it's fun!


Tryouts usually happen at the start of a school year or the start of a season.

Soccer

Our soccer team is a great opportunity for students who have experience in a soccer team outside the school. Team tryouts happen in the beginning of the school year. Seniors who perform outstandingly, will be competing in the provincials. Juniors will compete in Fraser Valley tournaments.


Coaches:

Mr. Sujan - sujan_j@surreyschools.ca

Mr. Green - green_ryan@surreyschools.ca

Basketball

The basketball team is a great opportunity for students who have experience in basketball. Team tryouts happen in the beginning of the school year. Seniors who perform outstandingly will be competing in the annual RCMP Basketball Tournament as well as the provincials. Juniors will compete in tournaments across the Fraser Valley.


Coaches:

Mr. Reid - reidg@surreyschools.ca

Robotics

The engineering department has a special team for students that are interested in robotics and VEX. The Robotics club is free to sign up for. They build and upgrade robots and take them to provincial VEX competitions. All grades are welcome!
