Soccer

Our soccer team is a great opportunity for students who have experience in a soccer team outside the school. Team tryouts happen in the beginning of the school year. Seniors who perform outstandingly, will be competing in the provincials. Juniors will compete in Fraser Valley tournaments.

Basketball

The basketball team is a great opportunity for students who have experience in basketball. Team tryouts happen in the beginning of the school year. Seniors who perform outstandingly will be competing in the annual RCMP Basketball Tournament as well as the provincials. Juniors will compete in tournaments across the Fraser Valley.
