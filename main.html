Welcome to Enver Creek Secondary's Website

Enver Creek Secondary, home of the Cougars, has been educating the Surrey youth since 1997. Our variety of departments and opportunities will help students, grade 8-12, learn and succeed.

Our mission is to provide students every opportunity to learn skills that will help them be prepared for post-secondary careers and post-secondary education.
