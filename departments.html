Departments

Enver Creek offers a variety of areas of study, while keeping on the mandatory curriculum. Students get to choose which area of curriculum they would like to pursue.

Math

Math is a crucial academic for students, as it teaches them everyday math skills, all the way to scientific calculus formulas. The math department also invites students who perform brilliant in math, to to join the math team that compete in math competitions.

English

English is a very important academic, as it teaches students about proper grammar, spelling and punctuation, as well as literature and poetry. Poetry contests provide students with interest in poetry, to express themseelves in a poem. This could also lead to a potential scholarship.

Science

Science educates students about our wonderful world. From exploding reactions, to airplane engineering, to cell mechanisms, science is quite an interesting subject.
